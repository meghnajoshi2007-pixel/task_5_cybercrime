Stay Safe Online – Prevention Checklist for College Students
 🔐 General Online Safety Tips

- Use strong and unique passwords for email, banking, and social media accounts.
- Enable two-factor authentication (2FA) on important accounts.
- Never click on suspicious links received via SMS, email, or WhatsApp.
- Always verify the sender’s email address before responding to urgent requests.
- Avoid downloading apps or APK files from unknown or unofficial sources.
- Keep your mobile phone, laptop, and apps updated regularly.
- Use privacy settings on social media to limit public access to your personal information.
- Never share OTPs, passwords, Aadhaar details, or personal information with anyone.

💳 Financial / UPI Safety Tips

- Remember: Entering your UPI PIN means you are sending money, not receiving it.
- Do not approve unknown “Collect Requests” in UPI apps.
- Verify customer care numbers only through official websites.
- Regularly check your bank statements for unauthorized transactions.
 
🚨 Reporting Cybercrime

If you experience cyber fraud:
- Immediately call **Helpline 1930** for financial fraud reporting.
- Report the incident at the **National Cyber Crime Portal: https://cybercrime.gov.in**
